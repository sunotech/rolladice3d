Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

Thank you for playing Roll A Dice 3D. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Privacy by Default

We believe in minimizing data collection. Roll A Dice 3D does not operate its own servers and we do not store your personal data. However, to support the app, we use third-party services that may collect data. This ensures transparency while clearly stating that we (the developers) are not tracking you personally.

Permissions

The app does not request any device permissions beyond basic functionality.

Third-Party Services

To support our development, we use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. This service may collect and use:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising IDs (e.g., IDFA) to serve relevant ads and prevent fraud.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (via IP address) for geographic ad targeting.
  • Usage Data: Interaction metrics (clicks, views) and app performance data.
  • Cookies/Local Storage: Technologies used for frequency capping and ad delivery.

For more details, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

Changes To Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email.
